it's fun to learn!

Anel is my mum. She takes me to London for fun and to work to give me a better education. I'm homeschooled and I DEFINITELY enjoy it a lot! We go to places like the Guardian headquarters to learn about coding, history, journalism, science, all the topics you could think of. We don't just go to London though! We go to places all over the UK! I give credit to the Guardian, who gave me the idea to start a blog online!
